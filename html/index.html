Meddy

A mobile pharmaceutecal app that can scan or search up any medicine approved for supply on the New Zealand Universal List of Medicines database. Our team worked on this app as a university project in collaboration with Medsafe, a medicial regulatory body run by the NZ Ministry of Health.
